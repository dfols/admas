About Page

Welcome to Admas, where the rich and vibrant traditions of Eritrea and Ethiopia come alive. Located in the heart of Columbus Ohio , our restaurant is a place where culture, community, and cuisine converge to create an unforgettable dining experience.

History/Story: Founded in 2023, Admas was born out of a deep passion for the flavors and heritage of Eritrea and Ethiopia. Our founder Biniam weldegebriel, wanted to share the warmth and richness of their homeland with the people of Columbus Ohio. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a beloved local institution, known for our authentic dishes and welcoming atmosphere.
